Selfie Swatch

Welcome to Selfie Swatch!

This app will guide you through capturing 5 color samples from your head and body, then create a beautiful portrait with a custom tiled background made from your colors.

Make sure you're in good lighting and your webcam is working.

Note: This app uses advanced AI body segmentation for precise background removal!
